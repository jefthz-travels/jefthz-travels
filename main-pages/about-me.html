ABOUT ME

HEY, I'M JEFTHZ!

At 26, I started to explore the different provinces of the Philippines. I am a backpacker, budget traveler, hugot lover, aspiring travel blogger and photographer. I love nature tripping, tree planting activities, mountaineering, island hopping, visiting historical and heritage sites. I am also a cactus and succulent lover. On weekdays, I worked as a Software Engineer but during weekends I continue to learn more about web development or having an out of town trip.

I want to travel and explore different places to experience new things, to be educated with different local cultures and way of living and to learn more about myself. It is my goal to explore and document my travel experience on the 81 provinces of the Philippines and be able to share my experience in the world. And that's why this website was created.
